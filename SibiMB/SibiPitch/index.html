Welcome to SibilanceAI

SibilanceAI is a research and development company that focuses on improving AI narrative generation, adding psychometrics to make the behavior of AI characters in video games more realistic, and creating interactive stories and video games.

Our team has developed novel psychometrics that can be used to enhance the believability and immersion of AI-powered storytelling and gaming experiences.
